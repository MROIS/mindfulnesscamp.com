ON THE BEAUTIFUL OREGON COAST!

A four day event for people from all walks of life, where adults and children can learn practical ways to live more peaceful, centered lives with each other and the natural world.

The weather should be warm and we will have 2 miles of wild Oregon coastline to explore, beach fires to sing around, delicious local foods to eat and comfortable, rustic cabins and lodges to stay in.

This event is being developed and hosted by interfaith organizations throughout Oregon and Washington. The retreat will be lead by Monastic Dharma Teachers from Deer Park Monastery. They have been ordained and trained by Zen Master Thich Nhat Hanh. A children’s program will be offered.

ABOUT WESTWIND

The 529 acre Westwind site is located within a United Nations Bio-Sphere which lies 50 miles due West of Salem on Oregon’s central coast. (We will pick you up by ferry and transport you across the Salmon River to Westwind).


Directions to Westwind.

COME JOIN US

Come join us in one of the most beautiful places in the world! We invite you to join us as we create a Mindful Community surrounded by this wild and beautiful coastline. We will learn practical ways to live more peaceful, centered and compassionate lives with each other and the natural world.

ACCOMODATIONS

American Camp style cabins are set in coastal forests surrounding the lodge, many with views of the ocean and beaches. They are all heated, well lit. Please let us know if you would like to reserve a cabin with your family or friends. There is also the option of camping in canvas tents in the dune area north of the lodge or you can bring your own tent. We are happy to help with any of your mobility and special sleeping needs contact us.
(Please see site maps and further photos at westwind.org)

MEALS

Our healthy cuisine will reflect what is fresh and seasonal to our local coastal area. Farmers in our food-shed are already planting veggies that will be providing us with special ingredients for our Vegan menu! We place a special emphasis on extraordinarily delicious and sustainable foods. We will be able to meet your special diet needs, contact us and send your requests to the event coordinator.

CROSSING TO THE SITE

Westwind is only approachable by water on our ferry. 2018 Crossing times for August 25 is at 2pm and on August 28 at 1pm. Please Arrive at 1PM to unload gear, park and join the group. Upon arrival at Westwind there is a short walk through the shore pine forest to the lodge. (Please contact us regarding alternative transportation to the site or if the scheduled Saturday crossing is not possible) We will e-mail all registered participants an equipment list to ensure a comfortable weekend at the coast.
